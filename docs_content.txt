Product Requirements Document (PRD)
CarbonWise
Version: 1.0
Status: Draft
1. Executive Summary
CarbonWise is an intelligent sustainability assistant that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce
their carbon footprint through personalized recommendations, behavioral insights, and measurable
sustainability goals.
Unlike traditional carbon calculators that simply estimate emissions, CarbonWise continuously analyzes
user behavior , identifies emission hotspots, and recommends simple, actionable changes that maximize
environmental impact while minimizing user effort.
The platform acts as a personal carbon coach that transforms sustainability data into practical decisions.
2. Problem Statement
Individuals contribute to carbon emissions through transportation, food consumption, energy usage, and
purchasing behavior , but most people:
Do not understand their personal carbon footprint
Cannot identify major emission sources
Lack personalized guidance
Struggle to maintain sustainable habits
Cannot measure progress effectively
As a result, sustainability awareness rarely translates into long-term behavior change.
3. Product Vision
Enable every individual to make environmentally responsible decisions through personalized carbon
intelligence.
